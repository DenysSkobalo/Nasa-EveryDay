What it is?

Is a service that can show you an article about what happened or what was discovered in our galaxy or on earth, on the day you chose.

How does this service work?

  1. 1. On the navigation bar, select "Application"
  2. 2. You will go to the "App" page, first you will see an example of what the "Article" will look like, pay attention to the lower right corner, you will see a button, click on it and a modal window will appear.
  3. 3. After clicking the button in the lower right corner, a modal window will appear in which you can select a date. After the selected date, click the Search button on the right to display the Article.

    4. How to close a modal window?

    To close the modal window, click anywhere in the black area

Errors are possible

Errors are possible on the website because the project is under development! But other errors are also possible:

  1. 1. If the image is not updated, try to refresh the page itself and select this date again, or wait.
Warning!

You cannot select a future date because there can be no future dates in the NASA archives.
